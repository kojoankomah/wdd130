Blue Lagoon Rafting

At Blue Lagoon, our purpose is to connect people with nature through thrilling and safe rafting experiences. Our mission is to provide unforgettable journeys down the world's most breathtaking rivers, fostering a spirit of adventure and environmental stewardship. We believe in the power of water to unite communities and inspire respect for our planet. Guided by our creed, "Adventure with Integrity," we prioritize safety, sustainability, and exceptional service in every expedition. Join us as we navigate the wild, embrace the rush, and create lasting memories. Together, let's make waves!

History

Founded in 1995 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts, Blue Lagoon began as a small family-run operation in the heart of the Rockies. What started as a single raft and a handful of guided tours quickly grew as word spread about our thrilling experiences and commitment to safety. Over the years, we've expanded our offerings to include diverse river routes and adventure packages, attracting both seasoned rafters and newcomers alike. Today, we pride ourselves on being a leader in the rafting community, dedicated to preserving the rivers we love and inspiring future generations to explore the great outdoors.
